Recycling Information

♲

PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)

Commonly found in:
    - Soft drink bottles
    - Water bottles
    - Ketchup bottles
    - Beer bottles
    - Mouthwash bottles
    - Peanut butter cantainers
    - Salad dressing bottles
    - Vegetable oil bottles
To Recycle:
    - Empty and rinse bottle and remove cap unless it states     otherwise before recycling.

♲

HDPE (High Density Polyethylene)

Commonly found in:
    - Milk jugs
    - Juice bottles
    - Bleach containers
    - Detergent containers
    - Shampoo bottles
    - Trash bags
    - Shopping bags
    - Motor oil bottles
    - Butter containers
    - Yogurt containers
    - Cereal box liners
To Recycle:
    - Some places may deny containers without necks. Plastic bags     cannot be recycled but some stores will accept them.

♲

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)

Commonly found in:
    - Blister packaging
    - Wire jacketing
    - Siding
    - Windows
    - Piping
To Recycle:
    - Accepted by some plastic lumber makers otherwise rarely     recycled.

♲

LDPE (Low Density Polyethylene)

Commonly found in:
    - Squeeszable bottles
    - Bread
    - Frozen food
    - Dry-cleaning
    - Shopping bags
    - Tote bags
    - Furniture
To Recycle:
    - Few communities accept this, otherwise throw in trash.

♲

PP (Polypropylene)

Commonly found in:
    - Some yogurt containers
    - Syrup bottles
    - Medicine bottles
    - Bottle caps
    - Straws
To Recycle:
    - Recycled through curbside programs, clean plastic of any food     and throw away cap.

♲

PS (Polystyrene)

Commonly found in:
    - Disposable plates and cups
    - Meat trays
    - Egg cartons
    - Carryout containers
    - Aspirin bottles
    - CD cases
To Recycle:
    - Rarely recycled otherwise throw away in trash.

♲

OTHER

Commonly found in:
    - Three to five gallon water bottles
    - Bulletproof materials
    - Sunglasses
    - DVDs
    - Clear plastic cutlery
    - Lighting fixtures
    - Signs and displays
    - Some food containers
    - Nylon
To Recycle:
    - Rarely recycled otherwise throw away.
